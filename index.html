VISION

GVT-VTC acts as a Centre of Excellence for Skill Development and will be Growth Engine for enhancing Sustainable Livelihood of Youth.

MISSION

Providing high quality employment oriented vocational training and employment assistance to unemployed youth to improve their socio-economic condition.

Benefits of VTC

  1. TO CONSTRUCTION INDUSTRY - Optimum utilization of materials and tech, consistent technology, increase in profitability and customer satisfaction and provides employee development and retension.
  2. TO THE TRAINEES- they recieve a government recognized ceritificate, improved work prospects and knowledge, recognition within industry, socio-economic development, better overseas opportunities, quick grasping of new tech and also helps increase the credentials to get loan for buisness 
  3. TO THE SOCIETY- There is an old proverb that says - "Give a person a bread and feed him for a day. Teach a person to earn a bread and you feed him for a lifetime." Training to more and more people will lead to a sustainable society.
